About ZenCV

ZenCV is the Infinite CV Factory specifically engineered for the global Indian professional. Our mission is to bridge the gap between Indian talent and international hiring standards.

Whether you are applying for a tech role in Silicon Valley, a management position in the UK, or a skilled trade in the Gulf, ZenCV provides the technical and strategic framework to ensure your profile is noticed by both robots (ATS) and humans (Recruiters).

Our platform combines data-driven structural compliance with high-aesthetic design, making it the most versatile CV tool in the market.
