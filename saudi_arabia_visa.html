🇸🇦 SAUDI ARABIA — WORK IMMIGRATION GUIDE FOR INDIANS

🔹 1. OVERVIEW

Saudi Arabia is one of the largest job markets in the Gulf region for Indian workers, especially in construction, oil & gas, healthcare, IT, and services. Due to large-scale infrastructure projects and economic diversification (Vision 2030), demand for foreign workers remains high. The country follows a sponsorship-based system (Kafala), and offers tax-free salaries but limited long-term settlement options.

🔹 2. TYPES OF WORK VISAS

1. Employment Visa (Work Visa) Most common visa for long-term employment.

Requires employer sponsorship and is the standard path for Indian professionals.

2. Temporary Work Visa For short-term projects and limited duration roles.
3. Premium Residency Special category for high-income individuals.

Allows more flexibility and mobility within the country, though limited to specific cases.

4. Seasonal Work Visa Short-term work specifically for Hajj and Umrah services.

🔹 3. ELIGIBILITY CRITERIA

  • ✔ Valid job offer from a registered Saudi employer
  • ✔ Age between 21–60 years
  • ✔ Relevant qualifications or skills for the position
  • ✔ Mandatory medical fitness (approved centers only)
  • ✔ Valid passport (minimum 6 months validity)
  • ✔ Clean criminal record (Police Clearance Certificate)

🔹 4. REQUIRED DOCUMENTS

  • 📄 Valid passport & Passport-size photographs
  • 📄 Job offer / Official employment contract
  • 📄 Work visa authorization from Saudi authorities
  • 📄 Attested educational certificates
  • 📄 Medical fitness certificate & Police clearance certificate

🔹 5. STEP-BY-STEP PROCESS

✅ A. Eligibility & Job Search

Identify the correct visa category. Secure a job offer via recruitment agencies or portals. Target employers authorized to sponsor foreign workers. This is the MOST IMPORTANT STEP.

✅ B. Employer Authorization

The employer applies through Saudi labor authorities to get your visa authorization. Once approved, you receive the visa authorization number required for stamping.

✅ C. Medicals & Visa Stamping

Complete a medical examination at approved centers. Submit your documents to the Saudi Embassy for visa stamping in your passport.

✅ D. Arrival & Iqama

Enter Saudi Arabia on your work visa. Your employer applies for your Iqama (Residence Permit), which serves as your legal residency and work authorization.

🧠 REALITY CHECK

❌ Beware of fake agents; always verify the Saudi employer.
❌ The system is strictly employer-controlled (Kafala).
❌ Permanent residency is not available through the standard route.
❌ Strict penalties apply for working illegally or for an unauthorized sponsor.

🔹 10. PROS & CONS

❌ CONS: No PR, job dependency, strict laws, limited job security.
