🇴🇲 OMAN — WORK IMMIGRATION GUIDE FOR INDIANS

🔹 1. OVERVIEW

Oman is a popular destination for Indian workers due to steady job demand, tax-free income, and relatively simple employer-driven visa processes. Opportunities are available in construction, oil & gas, healthcare, retail, hospitality, logistics, and services. Oman follows a sponsorship system for employment visas and offers limited long-term settlement options.

🔹 2. TYPES OF WORK VISAS

1. Employment Visa Most common visa, typically valid for 2 years.

Requires direct employer sponsorship and is the primary route for both skilled and unskilled labor.

2. Temporary Work Visa For short-term projects or specific tasks.

Linked to a specific duration and employer for urgent or temporary staffing needs.

3. Express Visa Fast-track processing for urgent travel.
4. Family Joining Visa For dependents of professionals working in Oman.

Requires the sponsor (worker) to meet a specific minimum salary threshold set by the government.

🔹 3. ELIGIBILITY CRITERIA

  • ✔ Valid job offer from a registered Omani employer
  • ✔ Age between 21–60 years (varies by profession)
  • ✔ Relevant qualifications or skills for the specific role
  • ✔ Mandatory medical fitness (GAMCA/Medical certification)
  • ✔ Valid passport (minimum 6 months validity)
  • ✔ Clean criminal record (Police Clearance Certificate)

🔹 4. REQUIRED DOCUMENTS

  • 📄 Valid passport & Passport-size photographs
  • 📄 Job offer / Official employment contract
  • 📄 Work visa approval from Oman authorities
  • 📄 Attested educational certificates (if required)
  • 📄 Medical fitness certificate & Police clearance certificate

🔹 5. STEP-BY-STEP PROCESS (DETAILED)

✅ A & B: Eligibility & Job Search THE MOST IMPORTANT STEP.

Secure a job offer via LinkedIn, Indeed Oman, or recruitment agencies. Ensure the employer is legally registered and authorized to sponsor foreign workers.

✅ C & D: Employer Authorization

The employer submits the application to Omani authorities. The government verifies the job role and company quota. Once approved, you receive the work visa approval document.

✅ E & F: Medicals & Entry

Complete your medical examination and attest your documents if required. Enter Oman using the approved entry visa within its validity period.

✅ G, H & I: Residence Card & Biometrics

After arrival, the employer applies for your Resident Card (Residence Card). You must complete biometrics (fingerprints and photos) for official records before you can legally start work.

🧠 REALITY CHECK

❌ Beware of fake agents charging illegal fees for visas.
❌ The system is strictly employer-controlled (Sponsorship).
❌ Permanent residency is not available for general workers.
❌ Strict penalties are imposed for working for a non-sponsor or working illegally.

🔹 10. PROS & CONS

✔ PROS: Tax-free salary, simple visa process, employer handles most steps, stable job market.

❌ CONS: No PR, job dependency, moderate salaries compared to other Gulf states, limited job mobility.
