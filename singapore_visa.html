🇸🇬 SINGAPORE — WORK IMMIGRATION GUIDE FOR INDIANS

🔹 1. OVERVIEW

Singapore is a major global financial and business hub, attracting Indian professionals due to its high salaries, tax advantages, and proximity to India. Key sectors include finance, IT, banking, logistics, healthcare, and hospitality. Singapore offers employer-sponsored work passes rather than traditional visas, and while it provides long-term stay options, obtaining Permanent Residency (PR) is selective.

🔹 2. TYPES OF WORK VISAS

1. Employment Pass (EP) Most common for professionals.

Requires job offer from Singapore employer. High salary threshold.

2. S Pass For mid-skilled workers.

Lower salary requirement than EP. Quota-based system.

3. Work Permit For semi-skilled or low-skilled workers.

Strict employer control. Common in construction, manufacturing.

4. EntrePass For entrepreneurs starting a business.

Requires business plan approval.

5. Personalised Employment Pass (PEP) For high-earning professionals.

More flexibility than EP.

🔹 3. ELIGIBILITY CRITERIA

  • ✔ Valid job offer (mandatory for most passes)
  • ✔ Relevant educational qualifications
  • ✔ Minimum salary requirement (varies by pass)
  • ✔ Work experience (preferred)
  • ✔ Employer eligibility and quota compliance
  • ✔ Clean legal and medical record

🔹 4. REQUIRED DOCUMENTS

  • 📄 Valid passport
  • 📄 Job offer letter
  • 📄 Educational certificates
  • 📄 Resume/CV
  • 📄 Work experience letters
  • 📄 Passport-size photographs
  • 📄 Employer details and company registration

🔹 5. STEP-BY-STEP PROCESS

✅ A. Check Eligibility for the Appropriate Work Pass

Professional → Employment Pass (EP) | Mid-level → S Pass | Low-skilled → Work Permit. Check if salary meets threshold and job role is eligible.

✅ B. Secure a Job Offer from Singapore Employer
The MOST IMPORTANT STEP.

Apply via LinkedIn, Indeed Singapore, or Company portals. Target companies that sponsor work passes and meet quota requirements.

✅ C. Employer Applies for Work Pass

Employer handles most of the process via the Ministry of Manpower (MOM). You do not apply directly in most cases.

✅ D. Receive In-Principle Approval (IPA)

Receive your IPA letter. This allows entry into Singapore and is valid for a limited time.

✅ E. Final Formalities in Singapore

Travel to Singapore, undergo medical examination (if required), and attend biometrics for pass issuance.

🧠 REALITY CHECK

❌ Applying without checking salary eligibility | ❌ Fake job offers | ❌ Ignoring quota restrictions | ❌ Weak CV

Note: PR is attractive but difficult and selective to obtain.

Processing Time

1–4 weeks (work pass approval)

Cost Estimate

Fee: SGD 105–225 (Usually paid by employer)

Salary Expectation

Entry: SGD 3k-5k | High: SGD 10k+/month

🔹 11. DIFFICULTY LEVEL

👉 MEDIUM

Getting a job is the key challenge; the visa process itself is very fast.
