IMMIGRATION PROCESS FOR INDIANS IN UAE

🇦🇪 UNITED ARAB EMIRATES — WORK IMMIGRATION GUIDE

🔹 1. Overview

The United Arab Emirates is one of the most popular destinations for Indian workers due to its tax-free income, high demand for labor, and fast visa processing system. Major opportunities exist in construction, hospitality, healthcare, IT, retail, and logistics. The UAE offers employer-sponsored work visas as well as newer self-sponsored options like freelance and Golden visas.

🔹 2. Types of Work Visas

1. Standard Employment Visa: Most common work visa. Requires employer sponsorship. Valid for 1–3 years.

2. Free Zone Work Visa: Issued by companies in UAE free zones. Simplified process. Limited to specific zones.

3. Green Visa: Self-sponsored visa (no employer required). For skilled professionals and freelancers.

4. Golden Visa: Long-term visa (5–10 years). For highly skilled professionals, investors.

5. Temporary / Part-Time Work Permit: Short-term or flexible work. Issued by UAE authorities.

🔹 3. Eligibility Criteria

  • Valid job offer (for employment visa)
  • Age between 18–60 years
  • Relevant educational qualifications
  • Medical fitness (mandatory)
  • Employer sponsorship approval
  • Valid passport (minimum 6 months validity)

🔹 4. Required Documents

  • Valid passport
  • Job offer / employment contract
  • Passport-size photographs
  • Educational certificates (attested if required)
  • Medical fitness report
  • Entry permit issued by authorities
  • Employer documents (company license, etc.)

🔹 5. STEP-BY-STEP PROCESS

A

Check Eligibility for the Appropriate Visa: You must first choose the correct visa type.

👉 Example: Job-based → Employment Visa | Freelancer → Green Visa | High-skilled → Golden Visa

What you check here: Do you have a valid job offer? Do you meet qualification requirements? Are your documents properly attested?

👉 Wrong selection can delay or reject your process.

B

Secure a Job Offer from UAE Employer: This is the MOST IMPORTANT STEP. Unlike the US/UK, UAE process is fully employer-driven.

What you must do: Apply via job portals (LinkedIn, Indeed UAE) or recruitment agencies. Target companies that provide visa sponsorship and are legally registered.

👉 Without job offer → no employment visa.

C

Employer Applies for Work Permit Approval: Now the employer starts the process through UAE labor authorities (MOHRE).

What happens: Employer applies for work permit quota approval, submits employment contract, and the government verifies job eligibility.

👉 Work permit must be approved before entry visa.

D

Receive Employment Entry Visa (Pink Visa): Once approved, you receive the entry visa (pink visa). It is usually valid for 60 days.

👉 This allows you to travel to UAE legally.

E

Travel to UAE: You enter the UAE using the entry visa. Now the real processing starts inside UAE.

F

Medical Fitness Test: Mandatory step after arrival. Includes a blood test and chest X-ray. Must be done at government-approved centers.

👉 If failed → visa rejected.

G

Apply for Emirates ID (Biometrics): Fingerprints and photographs are taken at the Emirates Identity Authority center.

👉 This ID is required for banking, renting a house, and legal identity.

H

Residence Visa Stamping: Now your residency is finalized. The residence visa is stamped in your passport and is valid for 1–3 years.

👉 Work permit is linked to this visa.

I

Work Permit Activation (Labour Card): Final step: Work permit (Labour Card) is issued and you can officially start working.

J

Start Working in UAE: Now you are legally allowed to work, open a bank account, rent accommodation, and sponsor family (if eligible).

🧠 REALITY CHECK (IMPORTANT)

This process is much easier than UK/USA, but: Fully dependent on employer. Fake job offers are common. Strict medical rules apply.

Common mistakes:

  • ❌ Going on tourist visa and working illegally
  • ❌ Fake agents
  • ❌ Not verifying company

👉 UAE has strict penalties for illegal work.

🔹 7. Cost Estimate

AED 3,000 – AED 7,000 (usually paid by employer). Medical + ID + documentation included.

🔹 6. Processing Time:
2–4 weeks (typical).

🔹 8. Salary Expectation

Construction/low-skill: AED 1,000 – 3,000/month
Mid-level: AED 3,000 – 10,000/month
Skilled: AED 10,000 – 30,000+/month

👉 Income is tax-free.

✔ Pros

Fast visa processing, Tax-free salary, High demand for workers, Employer handles process.

❌ Cons

No permanent residency, Job-dependent visa, Strict laws and penalties, Limited job security.

🔹 11. Difficulty Level: Easy to Medium

No traditional PR system. Golden Visa available for long-term stay.
