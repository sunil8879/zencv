🇲🇺 MAURITIUS — WORK IMMIGRATION GUIDE FOR INDIANS

🔹 1. OVERVIEW

Mauritius is an emerging destination for Indian professionals due to its stable economy, cultural similarities, and proximity to India. Opportunities exist in tourism, hospitality, finance, IT, education, and construction. Mauritius offers employer-sponsored work permits and combined residence permits, with some long-term stay options for skilled professionals and investors.

🔹 2. TYPES OF WORK VISAS

1. Occupation Permit (OP) Most common permit combining work and residence.

For professionals, investors, and self-employed individuals. Requires meeting specific salary or investment thresholds.

2. Work Permit Employer-sponsored for standard employment.

Usually issued for short to medium-term roles in sectors like construction and manufacturing.

3. Residence Permit Issued for long-term stay.

Often linked with an occupation permit or significant investment in the country.

4. Premium Visa For remote workers and long-stay tourists.

Allows a stay of up to one year but does not permit entry into the local Mauritian labor market.

🔹 3. ELIGIBILITY CRITERIA

  • ✔ Valid job offer (mandatory for Work Permits and OPs)
  • ✔ Relevant educational qualifications and work experience
  • ✔ Minimum salary threshold (specifically for Occupation Permits)
  • ✔ Valid passport & Medical fitness certification
  • ✔ Clean criminal record (Police Clearance Certificate)

🔹 4. REQUIRED DOCUMENTS

  • 📄 Valid passport & Passport-size photographs
  • 📄 Job offer / Official employment contract
  • 📄 Educational certificates & Work experience letters
  • 📄 Medical certificate & Police clearance certificate

🔹 5. STEP-BY-STEP PROCESS

✅ A. Eligibility & Job Offer
THE MOST IMPORTANT STEP.

Secure a job offer via LinkedIn, Indeed Mauritius, or direct portals. Ensure the employer is willing to provide permit support.

✅ B. Employer Application

The employer submits your application to the Economic Development Board (EDB). They verify the job role, salary, and company credentials.

✅ C. Approval & Travel

Once you receive "Approval in Principle," you can travel to Mauritius to complete post-arrival formalities.

✅ D. Post-Arrival Formalities

Complete a local medical check-up and register with authorities. Your physical Occupation Permit or Work Permit card is then issued.

🧠 REALITY CHECK

❌ Beware of fake job offers; verify the company through official channels.
❌ The job market is smaller than major EU or Gulf destinations.
❌ Salaries are moderate compared to Western countries.
❌ Permanent Residency is limited mainly to high-end investors and long-term professionals.
