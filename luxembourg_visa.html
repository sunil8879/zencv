🇱🇺 LUXEMBOURG — WORK IMMIGRATION GUIDE FOR INDIANS

🔹 1. Overview

Luxembourg is a small but highly developed European nation known for its strong economy, especially in finance, banking, IT, and logistics. It offers high salaries and a high standard of living, but has a limited job market and strict immigration procedures. Work visas are mainly employer-sponsored, and long-term residency is possible.

🔹 2. Types of Work Visas

1. Short-Stay Visa (Type C): For business trips or short-term work (up to 90 days).

2. Long-Stay Work Visa (Type D): For employment exceeding 90 days. Requires job offer.

3. EU Blue Card: For highly skilled professionals. Higher salary threshold required.

4. Intra-Company Transfer Visa: For employees transferred within a company.

🔹 3. Eligibility Criteria

  • Valid job offer from Luxembourg employer
  • Work authorization approved by government
  • Relevant qualifications
  • Work experience (role-dependent)
  • Clean criminal record
  • Medical fitness

🔹 4. Required Documents

  • Valid passport
  • Employment contract
  • Work authorization approval
  • Educational certificates
  • Work experience proof
  • Medical certificate
  • Police clearance certificate

🔹 5. STEP-BY-STEP PROCESS

✅ A

Check Eligibility for the Appropriate Visa: You must first identify the correct visa type.

👉 Example: High-skilled → EU Blue Card | Regular job → Long-Stay Work Visa

What you check here: Salary threshold eligibility, Qualification requirements, and Job contract validity.

👉 Wrong category → rejection

✅ B

Secure a Job Offer from Luxembourg Employer: This is the MOST IMPORTANT STEP. You cannot enter Luxembourg for work without a job offer.

What you must do: Apply via LinkedIn, Indeed Europe, or Company websites. Target companies that hire international workers and offer sponsorship.

👉 Without job → process stops

✅ C

Employer Applies for Work Authorization: Before visa, the employer applies to Luxembourg authorities.

👉 Mandatory approval required.

✅ D

Receive Temporary Authorization: Once approved, you receive official authorization.

👉 Required before visa application.

✅ E

Apply for Long-Stay Visa: Apply through Luxembourg Embassy. Submit application, provide all documents, and pay visa fee.

✅ F - I

Travel & Formalities: Enter the country. Within 3 days, register with local authorities. Undergo mandatory medical examination and apply for your physical residence card.

✅ J

Start Working: Now you can work legally, open a bank account, and settle in Luxembourg.

🧠 REALITY CHECK (IMPORTANT)

Job market is small and competitive. Language (French/German) often required. High salary but high living cost.

Common mistakes:

  • ❌ Applying without job offer
  • ❌ Ignoring language requirements
  • ❌ Weak CV

🔹 6. Processing Time: 4–12 weeks (approx)

🔹 7. Cost Estimate: Visa fee: €80–€100

🔹 8. Salary Expectation:
Finance/IT: €50,000 – €100,000/year
Entry-level: €30,000 – €50,000/year

🔹 9. PR Options: Apply after 5 years. Must meet residency and integration requirements.

🔹 10. Pros & Cons

✔ Pros: Very high salaries, Strong economy, EU mobility.

❌ Cons: Limited jobs, High cost of living, Language barrier.

🔹 11. Difficulty Level: Medium to Hard
