Privacy Policy

1. Data Protection

ZenCV respects your professional data. We do not store your CV information on our servers permanently. Once you download your file or close your browser tab, the session data is cleared. We use local browser storage to ensure your privacy.

2. Personal Information

We do not sell, rent, or share your email address or WhatsApp contact details with third-party recruiters, marketing agencies, or data brokers. Your contact information is used strictly for communication you initiate.

3. Document Security

The PDF generation happens locally within your browser environment whenever possible, ensuring that your sensitive work history remains under your control.

Global Compliance Standards

To protect our international users, ZenCV aligns its privacy framework with the world's strictest data laws:

GDPR (General Data Protection Regulation)

This is the European Union's standard for data privacy. It gives you the "Right to be Forgotten" and ensures that companies cannot collect your data without explicit consent. ZenCV follows GDPR by not tracking your personal professional history.

CCPA (California Consumer Privacy Act)

This is the North American standard (USA) for consumer privacy. It requires businesses to be transparent about what data is collected and gives users the right to opt-out of the sale of their personal information. ZenCV complies by having a "Zero-Sale" policy for all user metadata.
